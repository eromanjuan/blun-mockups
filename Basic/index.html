About BLUN

Building healthy, productive communities through education and human services.

Your Partner in Lifelong Learning

BLUN Educational Foundation Inc. is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing educational and human services programming to youth, adults, and organizations. We believe in the power of education to transform lives and build stronger communities.

Located in Guimba, Nueva Ecija, we offer accessible, high-quality programs that prepare students for meaningful careers in tourism, hospitality, and beyond.

  • 15+ years of service
  • Industry-aligned curriculum
  • Supportive learning environment
